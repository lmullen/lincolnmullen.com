Greetings. I am a historian of American religion and the nineteenth-century United States, often using computational methods for texts and maps.

I serve as the director of the [Roy Rosenzweig Center for History and New Media](https://rrchnm.org/), a research center which creates educational resources, data-driven histories, and other open-access digital work for public and scholarly audiences. I am also a [professor](https://historyarthistory.gmu.edu/people/lmullen) in the Department of History and Art History at George Mason University.
